Paddle GO – Account and Data Deletion

This page explains how users of the Paddle GO mobile application can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to: help.paddlego@outlook.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include the email address associated with your Paddle GO account.

Our team will process your deletion request as soon as possible.

Data That Will Be Deleted

When your request is processed, the following information will be removed from our systems:

  • User account information
  • Profile or account settings
  • App usage data associated with the account

Data Retention

Some information may be temporarily retained if required for:

  • Legal obligations
  • Fraud prevention
  • Security and abuse prevention

Any retained data will only be kept for the minimum time required for these purposes.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 7 days.
